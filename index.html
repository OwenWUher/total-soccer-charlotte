ABOUT US

More Than a Field. It's a Community.

Total Soccer Charlotte is an 11,000-square-foot field-turfed indoor soccer facility in the heart of Matthews, NC — a brand-new walled-in field with multiple-sized goals, parent bleachers, a sound system, and bathrooms. A place where families, friends, and competitors of every age and skill level can play the game they love, all year round, rain or shine.

From first-time youth players to seasoned adult league veterans, we're built around one idea: bringing people together through soccer. Reserve the field, throw a birthday party, or join a league — we'll make sure you feel right at home.
